About me

Hi my name is Logan Berggren. I am currently a student at the University of Nebraska Lincoln where I study Graphic Design and Advertising/Public Relations. ​ I love designing odd or 'out of the box' pieces. When creating, I want people to laugh. I also believe that art and design can change the world. So I love to work with nonprofits or create pieces for issues that I believe in. ​ Outside of the classroom, I spend my free time either crocheting, thrifting, or visiting a local coffee shop. I hope you enjoy my work and feel free to reach out to me on any of my socials :)
