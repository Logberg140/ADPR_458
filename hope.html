Hope Connecting Hearts

Hope Connecting Hearts Nigeria is a nonprofit that aims to provide necessary resources such as education and clean water to remote parts of Nigeria. I was tasked with updating and redesigning their entire website. I also was the photographer for their 2023 annual fundraiser.

I wanted to create a friendly user interface so people can quickly learn more about the organization, donate, or get involved.
