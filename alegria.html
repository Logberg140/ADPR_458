Alegria

Manifesto


Kinship is a way of life for Brazilians. The affection Brazilians have for one another stretches beyond close loved ones and reaches every corner of their community. Kinship is ingrained in Brazilian culture, and it’s ingrained in Alegria.

Alegria knows that acts of love spread joy amongst one’s kin. They create a sense of real appreciation. These authentic moments are key to c reating a community filled with joy. Essential to a life filled with laughter and happiness. It’s our mission to not only craft a beverage reminiscent of Brazilian kinship, but to inspire others to form these genuine connections daily.

We’re kin at Alegria, and we don’t just believe in joyful moments, we live them every day.

Together. Discover joy in one another.
